About Gaoqi


Gaoqi is made of 50% tech geek and 50% adrenaline junkie. He loves good experience in life in every respect, so he wants to bring good experiences for the lives of others as well. He is into challenging things. To him, things are not fun if they are too easy. He considers himself a generalist, and he is trying to build his T-shaped skillset with breadth of knowledge + depth of expertise (Jack of all trades, master of few). Take a glance at his life, he is into interesting creatures (bio), technologies, arts, and of course, adventures!

DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

Design is a blend of art and science. Design answers two questions: How things look (frontend) and how things work (backend). Design takes care of both external appearance and internal functionality, and combine these two aspects into a smooth and meaningful experience.
Good design is obvious. Great design is transparent.
Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication.
