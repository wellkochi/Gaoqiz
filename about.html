About Gaoqi

Gaoqi is an User Experience Designer / Interaction designer with front-end knowledge. He loves good experience in life in every respect, so he wants to design good experiences for the lives of others as well. His works are mainly based on the U.S and China, but he identifies himself as a citizen of planet earth. Take a glance at his life, he is into technology, art, and all kinds of adventures.

DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

Design is a blend of science and art. Design answers two questions: how things work(back-end)? How things look(front-end)? Design takes care of both internal functionality and external appearance. To combine these two aspects into a smooth and meaningful experience, that is what designers do. Good design is obvious. Great design is transparent.
Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication.
